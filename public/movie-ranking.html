Top Five English Movies I've Seen This Month

I Would Highly Recommend These Movies To Any And Everyone

Fight Club

A hyper masculine si-fi drama that makes some strong observations about modern society

Django Unchained

Gotta be one the best of Quinten Tarintino as he delivers one of the best action dramas starring Jamie Fox about a runaway slave becoming a bounty hunter

Rocky III

Definitely the most underrated rocky movie, apollo and rocky's bromance and training together is amazing and the story about hunger for success once you get it is amazing

Creed

Sylvester Stallone kills it in this, simply put its amazing

Rocky IV

A rollercoaster of ride of emotions drama and solid training, gotta be the best rocky film out there
